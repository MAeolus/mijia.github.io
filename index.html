About Me

I am a PhD student working on medical image analysis, with a particular focus on multimodal medical image registration, dense representation learning, and robust correspondence estimation under modality gaps and partial anatomical overlap.

My current research aims to learn registration-oriented representations that are invariant to modality-specific appearance changes while preserving spatial discriminability for dense anatomical correspondence.
